### Survey, panels, and lectures - Contributions score
One direction we envision this website to grow into is that while this websites create value for the academics students, it also can create values for the researchers. This can grow into a marketplace where lecturers can contribute their knowledge with the return of the students helping to disseminate the questionnaires for them to reach the targeted audience who fulfilled the criteria. 

Students can help their fellow students to fill up survey to gain contributions score. Meanwhile, they can use this to access some of the lectures. Or the personal consultation of the lecturers. Lecturers can look at the questionnaire and give comment on the quality.
